Chocolate Cake Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 cups sugar
  • 1 ¾ cups flour
  • ¾ cup cocoa powder
  • 1 ½ tsp baking powder
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup milk

Steps to Make Chocolate Cake

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Mix dry ingredients in a bowl.
  3. Add wet ingredients and mix until smooth.
  4. Pour batter into greased pans.
  5. Bake for 30-35 minutes.
  6. Let the cake cool before serving.

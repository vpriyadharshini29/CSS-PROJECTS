Delicious Pancake Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of Flour
  • 2 tbsp of Sugar
  • 1/2 cup of Milk
  • 2 Eggs
  • 1 tsp of Vanilla Extract
  • 1/4 tsp of Salt

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Mix all the dry ingredients in a bowl.
  3. Add the wet ingredients and mix well.
  4. Pour the batter into a greased baking pan.
  5. Bake for 30 minutes or until golden brown.
  6. Let it cool for 10 minutes before serving.


Delicious Chocolate Cake

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of Cocoa Powder
  • 2 cups of Sugar
  • 2 cups of Flour
  • 1/2 cup of Butter
  • 3 Eggs
  • 1 tsp of Baking Powder
  • 1/2 cup of Milk

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a large bowl, mix dry ingredients.
  3. Add wet ingredients and stir until smooth.
  4. Pour batter into a greased pan.
  5. Bake for 35-40 minutes or until a toothpick comes out clean.
  6. Let it cool before frosting and serving.
